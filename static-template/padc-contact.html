Quick answers

Frequently requested information.

How do I join PaDC as a driver?
Start by joining the driver interest list. You'll receive information, invitations to upcoming driver meetings, and priority notification when founding membership opens ahead of our October 2026 launch.
How can my organization partner with PaDC?
Use our partner inquiry form or email partners@padc.coop directly. We welcome conversations with funders, cooperative development organizations, workforce development agencies, and community partners.
When is PaDC launching?
PaDC's pilot launch target is October 1, 2026. We are currently in the organizing and membership recruitment phase. Join the driver interest list to receive updates as we build toward launch.
How can I invite PaDC to speak at an event?
PaDC's organizing team is available for community presentations, panel discussions, and information sessions. Use the contact form above or email info@padc.coop with your event details and we'll be in touch.
Is PaDC looking for volunteers or organizing help?
Yes. PaDC welcomes individuals who want to support our organizing work — including drivers who want to help recruit others, community members who can support outreach, and professionals with experience in cooperatives, workforce development, or community organizing. Let us know in the contact form above.
